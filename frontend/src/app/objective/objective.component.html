About us

Objective

The primary objective of Campus Compass, is to enhance the campus experience of B.K. Birla College for students, faculty, and visitors by providing an intuitive and accessible campus navigation system. By bridging the information gap between individuals and physical campus spaces, we've made it easier for users to locate academic buildings, administrative offices, event venues, and other essential points of interest.

Key Objectives:

01

Efficient Navigation

Provide accurate and efficient guidance to desired destinations within the campus.

02

Enhanced User Experience

Offer a user-friendly interface with features like search functionality, and feedback mechanisms.

03

Sustainability

Reduce reliance on printed maps and signage, contributing to a more environmentally friendly campus.
