User Manual

How to Use the Campus Compass

Welcome to the Campus Compass user guide. This manual will help you navigate our interactive campus map system effectively.

1

Accessing the Map

  • The Campus Compass is embedded within the official website and can be accessed directly from the designated section.
  • If the map fails to load, please ensure your internet connection is active and refresh the page.
2

Navigating the Map

  • The map provides a comprehensive bird's-eye view of the campus, displaying all key locations including classrooms, departments, libraries, cafeterias, and administrative offices.
  • Zoom functionality is available using the scroll wheel on your mouse or pinch gesture on touchscreen devices.
  • Click and drag to pan and explore different areas of the campus map.
3

Searching for Locations

  • Utilize the search bar to efficiently locate specific areas such as lecture halls, faculty rooms, or campus amenities.
  • Simply enter the name of a building or facility, and the map will highlight its location for your convenience.
4

Getting Directions

  • Select a specific location on the map to generate directions from your current position.
  • The advanced wayfinding feature will guide you through the optimal route with precise step-by-step navigation.
5

Using Map Layers

  • The system allows for seamless switching between different floors or views.
  • As the campus features multiple levels, please utilize the floor selection options for comprehensive navigation.
6

Troubleshooting

  • If you encounter loading issues, kindly clear your browser cache and reload the page.
  • For persistent technical difficulties, please contact the IT support department.

Need Further Assistance?

If you have any questions or require additional help, please contact our support team.
